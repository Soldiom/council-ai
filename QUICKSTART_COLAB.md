- Colab FREE has 12-hour limit
- **Solution:** Upgrade to Colab PRO ($10/month) for 24-hour sessions
- Or: Just restart the notebook (data is saved)

### **"GPU quota exceeded"**
- Colab FREE has usage limits
- **Solution:** Wait a few hours or upgrade to PRO

### **"API key invalid"**
- Check your HuggingFace token is correct
- Get new token: https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens
- Make sure token has WRITE permission
